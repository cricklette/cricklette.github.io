about me

Welcome, dear friend. I am Ella Lincoln, known more commonly online as Cricklette. I am a young freelance digital illustrator from the United States. My specialty is drawing detailed pieces of art using vibrant colors or in a dark-art inked style. Every art I make has a meaning or a purpose, and I especially love creating art for others to appreciate and enjoy. Immerse yourself in wonder!

  • Price varies with apparel/accents.

  • Utilizing PayPal for USD payments.
